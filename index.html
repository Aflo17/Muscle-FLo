Frequently asked questions

How do I sign in to MuscleFLo? ›

MuscleFLo uses Google or Apple Sign-In — no password required. On the login screen, tap "Continue with Google" or "Continue with Apple" to get started.

How do I connect with my trainer? ›

Your trainer adds you to their client list from their side of the app. Once they add your email address, you'll automatically appear in their client dashboard. No action needed from you.

Why am I not seeing my assigned workout? ›

Workouts are assigned by your trainer for specific dates. Check the Sessions screen and make sure you're viewing the correct day. Pull down to refresh if you've just been assigned one.

How do push notifications work? ›

MuscleFLo sends push notifications when your trainer assigns a new workout or meal plan. Make sure notifications are enabled for MuscleFLo in your iPhone Settings → Notifications.

How do I log a completed workout? ›

From your dashboard, tap today's workout card to open it. Log your sets and reps as you go, then tap "Complete Workout" when you're done. Your trainer will be notified automatically.

How do I submit a check-in? ›

Tap "Check-In" from the bottom navigation. Fill in your weekly metrics — weight, energy, sleep, notes — and optionally attach a progress photo. Tap Submit to send it to your trainer.

I'm a trainer. How do I add clients? ›

From the Clients tab, tap the + button and enter your client's email address. They'll be added to your roster immediately once they have a MuscleFLo account.

How do I delete my account? ›

Contact us at aflores@dreamsflo.com with your account email. We'll permanently delete your account and all associated data within 30 days.

What is the community feed? ›

The community feed lets you share your completed workouts with others in your trainer's group. When you finish a workout, you'll have the option to post it to the feed. Your post will include your workout name, sets, volume, and exercise thumbnails.

Can I delete a post from the community feed? ›

Yes. Tap the three-dot menu on any post you authored and select Delete. Your post will be removed immediately.

Who can see my messages? ›

Messages are private between you and your trainer only. No other users can see your conversations.

How do I manage my trainer subscription? ›

Trainer subscriptions are managed through Apple. Go to your iPhone Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions to view, change, or cancel your MuscleFLo subscription at any time.
